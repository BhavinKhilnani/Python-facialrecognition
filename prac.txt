- Cards -> class
    - Rank
        - ranks are Ace, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, Jack, Queen, and King
        - rank 8 cards are jokers ie. they can be played 
    - Suit
        -suits are hearts, diamonds, clubs, and spades
- Deck -> class
    - Holds the cards
    - distributes the cards to the players
    - puts a card in the discarded pile to start the game 
- Drawing pile -> class/array
    - holds the remaining cards
    - allows players to draw cards if they cant play a card 
- Two players
    - Hold cards -> array/list
    - Discard -> players can discard cards on the discard pile if the card which they want to discard has the same rank or suit as the card which is on top of the discard pile 
    - what if they cant play a card ?
        -they can draw upto 3 cards from the Drawing pile 
        -if they do find a playable card , they have to play it 
        -if they dont find a playable card , the turn passes to the next player 
    - track the turn 
    -check if a player ran out of cards to play 
         -if not then the game continues 
         -else player has won the game
-Discard pile
    -the discarded cards go to the bottom of Drawing pile
